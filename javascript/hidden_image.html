Fender Player Stratocaster

The Fender Player Stratocaster, released in 2018, is making waves in the guitar world with its classic design, superior craftsmanship, and outstanding sound quality. Its sleek and ergonomic feel is the result of an alder body, a modern "C"-shaped maple neck, and a 22-fret pau ferro fingerboard. The guitar comes with three Player Series Alnico 5 Strat single-coil pickups that provide dynamic and crystal-clear tones perfect for a range of genres, from rock to jazz. Boasting additional premium features, the Fender Player Stratocaster ensures exceptional tuning stability and effortless playing performance. No wonder it's a top choice for both novice and experienced guitarists who value a high-quality instrument at an affordable price.

Gibson Les Paul Standard '60s

For those who are looking for a guitar that blends vintage vibes with modern functionality, Gibson Les Paul Standard '60s can deliver you both. Released in 2019, this guitar is a nod to the original Les Paul models from the 1960s, featuring a mahogany body with a maple top, a slim taper 60s-style mahogany neck, and a rosewood fingerboard. The Burst bucker 61R and 61T humbucker pickups produce rich and warm tones that sustain beautifully. The tune-o-Matic bridge and stop bar tailpiece, Grover Robomatix tuners, and advanced electronics with a treble bleed circuit round out the guitar's impressive features. For guitarists who want to pay homage to the classics while enjoying modern functionality, the Gibson Les Paul Standard '60s is a top choice.
